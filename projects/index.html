Film Locations in San Francisco

Author: Xiaoshi Lu

San Francisco has appeared in many films, but the locations are not evenly spread across time or space. This article uses the Film Locations in San Francisco dataset to show when film-location records appear, where selected records are located, and which film-related organizations or people appear most often.

The main interactive visualization is Figure 2. It lets the reader choose a neighborhood and decade, then compares the number of records by decade with the actual coordinate points available for that selected group. This makes the dataset easier for a public reader to explore without needing to read the raw table.

Figures 1 and 3 provide context. Figure 1 shows how the number of records changes by release year, while Figure 3 summarizes common production companies, directors, and distributors. Together, the three figures connect time, place, and film-production attributes.
